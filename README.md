# ZeroLog

**Vehicle maintenance tracker — track services, parts, and part numbers across all your vehicles.**

Free. No ads. No account required. Your data stays on your device.

---

## Install on Android

1. Open **Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Go to **https://twelvesec72-cpu.github.io/zerolog/**
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner
4. Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **Install**

ZeroLog now appears in your app drawer and home screen just like any other app.

## Install on iPhone

1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Go to **https://twelvesec72-cpu.github.io/zerolog/**
3. Tap the **Share button** (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **Add**

ZeroLog now appears on your home screen and launches full-screen.

---

## Features

- **Multiple vehicles** — add as many as you need, each with their own color tag
- **Service history** — log maintenance by date and mileage with costs and notes
- **Parts registry** — save part numbers by category for quick lookup at the store
- **Parts in service logs** — attach existing or new parts when logging a service, auto-saved to your parts list
- **Global search** — search across all vehicles for any part, part number, or service record
- **Tap to copy** — tap any part number to copy it to your clipboard
- **CSV export/import** — back up your data or transfer to a new device
- **Dark and light mode**
- **Old Man Mode** — larger fonts for easier reading
- **Works offline** — no internet needed after first install

## Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection.

To transfer your data to a new phone, use **Settings → Export Data** to download a CSV backup, then **Settings → Import Data** on the new device.
